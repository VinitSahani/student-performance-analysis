Project Objectives & Scope

As part of the 2nd-year CS internship requirement, this analysis project operates under strict scope limits:

  • Data Cleaning: Differentiating between low performers and students who missed their final exam.
  • Correlation Finding: Quantifying the impact of school attendance on grade outcomes.
  • Visual Reporting: Rendering interactive dashboard distribution, pie, bar, and scatter charts.
  • Strict Exclusions: No Machine Learning, No Deep Learning, No SQL databases, No Power BI, and No Authentication.
